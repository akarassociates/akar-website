About Us

Our Journey

AKAR & Associates is a professionally managed firm of Chartered Accountants established in 1982.

1982 – Krishna Swarup Agrawal formed “K.S. Agrawal & Co.”

2008 – Four partners merged their practices to form AKAR & Associates (Aditya Agrawal, K.S. Agrawal, Annu Thapar, Rasik Makkar)

Present – Six partners with a multi-disciplinary team offering services across India

What We Do

The firm offers a comprehensive range of services in the following areas:

Audit & Assurance

Statutory audits, tax audits, internal and process audits, and certifications.

Tax Advisory & Litigation

Income Tax, TDS, GST, representation before appellate authorities.

Valuation & Compliance

Business valuation, registered valuers, SEBI, RBI, FEMA matters.

Business Advisory

Company Law, transaction structuring, feasibility studies, and more.
